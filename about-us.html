Pale Blue Dots


We are a group of 6 Code Institue students, past & present that came together to save earth over the course of 1 Hackathon!


Our Mission: Change Behaviours Through Terraforming


If we could show how much effort goes into creating an amazing unique planet like earth, maybe, just maybe, people would be reminded to appreciate the Pale Blue Dot we live on.


The idea was bold, the code was a challenge, but we had fun and learnt a tonne of crazy terraforming facts...... We hope we succeeded?
